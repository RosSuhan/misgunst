4x4 Adventures

The Vleesbaai 4x4 Dune Route is a must-visit for off-road enthusiasts, offering a thrilling 12 km circular route near Mossel Bay on the Garden Route. Known for its breathtaking coastal views and pristine sand dunes, this track provides a unique opportunity to hone your dune and deep sand driving skills in a stunning environment. With no encroaching bushes to scratch your vehicle and well-marked routes that shift with the wind, the experience is both challenging and rewarding.

Whether you're there for the thrill or to enjoy a family outing, the route offers plenty of play areas and picnic spots, including access to a beautiful beach. Tire pressure management is crucial, especially when tackling the famous "Dune"â€”0.8 bars is recommended for the best chance of success.

You won't be left stranded, as owner Riekie van Rensburg is always just a call away to assist if needed. Vleesbaai 4x4 is one of the few coastal trails that allows dune driving so close to the beach, making it an unforgettable adventure.
