Escape to Nature at Andries's Nook

Nestled in the heart of Misgunst Farm, Andries's Nook offers a peaceful retreat where nature lovers and adventure seekers can find solace. Surrounded by the tranquil beauty of the Western Cape, this rustic and eco-friendly cabin is a haven for those looking to recharge in a setting rich with natural wonders. Built primarily from wood and sink, the Nook seamlessly blends with its surroundings, offering an authentic connection to the landscape.

Cozy Comfort and Outdoor Living

Wake up to the sounds of nature in a well-equipped, comfortable cabin just 800 meters from the serene shores of Vleesbaai. Inside the Nook, you'll find a cozy double bed, as well as a kitchenette stocked with everything you need to prepare home-cooked meals. For those who love to cook outdoors, the Nook features a braai (BBQ) area, perfect for enjoying meals in the fresh air.
The outdoor shower, located next to the Nook, provides a private yet immersive experience. Whether you're washing off after a beach day or an early morning jog, you'll feel truly close to nature as the wind rustles through the surrounding vegetation and the waves crash in the distance.

Glamping in Style

For families or groups, Andries's Nook offers the option to add a unique glamping experience. The Nook sleeps two, but you can expand your stay with a glamping tent, set on a deck just five meters from the cabin. Equipped with two single beds, it's the perfect addition for those traveling with friends or family, bringing you even closer to the elements while maintaining a sense of comfort and privacy.

Peace and Adventure Combined

Andries's Nook is the ideal base for both relaxation and exploration. Whether you prefer peaceful walks, stargazing, or lounging on the deck with a book, this farm stay provides plenty of opportunities to unwind. For the more adventurous, activities such as 4x4 trails, fishing, and discovering tidal pools are just a stone's throw away. With hiking trails and Fransmanshoek's rugged beauty nearby, every day brings a new way to connect with nature.
