About Hendrick's Camp

Experience the beauty of nature at Hendrik's Camp, where you can set up camp and enjoy stunning views of Vleesbaai. This unique accommodation offers multiple camping facilities, perfect for families and groups looking to reconnect with the great outdoors. Each stand accommodates up to six guests, allowing you to bring your own tent or caravan, making it an ideal getaway for nature lovers.

Hendrik's Camp boasts solar power and gas geysers, ensuring a sustainable stay while providing essential comforts. Guests can enjoy the communal ablutions and covered lapa area, equipped with sinks and refrigerators, perfect for food prep and social gatherings. Each camping site is thoughtfully separated, providing a sense of privacy, while BBQ facilities are available for private use, allowing you to savor delicious meals under the stars.

Wake up to the sounds of nature and breathtaking sunrises from your stand, with easy access to the popular Vleesbaai beach, rock-fishing spots at Fransmanshoek, and beautiful Kanon beach for swimming and exploring rock pools. With a plethora of activities nearby, including hiking, bird watching, and sandboarding, you'll find adventure awaits at every turn. Plus, don't forget to spot whales from May to October!
