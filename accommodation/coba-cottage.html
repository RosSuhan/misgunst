About Coba Cottage

Nestled within the stunning landscape of Misgunst Farmstay, Coba Cottage is a cozy stand-alone cottage perfect for families or close friends seeking adventure and relaxation. Accommodating up to five guests, this charming retreat invites you to disconnect from the hustle and bustle of daily life and immerse yourself in nature.

Equipped with solar electricity and gas-powered amenities, the cottage offers essential comforts while remaining eco-friendly. The well-appointed kitchen includes a fridge, stove, oven, cookware, utensils, and dishes, ensuring you have everything you need to prepare meals during your stay. The inviting lounge area features a sleeper couch, perfect for a child, and all bedding and linens are provided (please remember to bring your own beach towels).

Step outside to the patio, where you can enjoy breathtaking sunset views or unwind with a good book. The space also includes a BBQ, making it ideal for late afternoon or evening gatherings around the fire. Ample open space surrounds the cottage, providing children with plenty of room to run and play, all while being close to nature.

Easily accessible by any type of vehicle, with a range of outdoor activities like 4x4 driving, fishing, and bird watching nearby, this is the perfect getaway for those looking to create lasting memories with loved ones.
