Dragon Dune

South Africa's Ultimate Sandboarding Experience

Dragon Dune, situated on private land, offers the longest and largest sandboarding ride in South Africa. Catering to everyone from beginners to pros, this massive dune provides a thrilling experience similar to snowboarding. Whether you're a corporate group, school team, or adventure-seeking family, Dragon Dune guarantees fun for all ages.

Don't worry if standing up isn't your thingâ€”sit-down and belly-board options ensure everyone enjoys the ride. Each sandboarding session lasts around 3 hours, and bookings are required a day in advance. All gear is provided, and while most trips take place in the morning, afternoon sessions can be arranged.

The exclusivity of Dragon Dune means no crowds and perfectly smooth slopes for maximum speeds. This world-famous dune has even been featured on major global platforms like CNN, BBC World, National Geographic, and South African shows like Top Billing and Boer Soek 'n Vrou.

If you're ready for an adventure unlike any other, Dragon Dune is waiting to provide you with a sandboarding experience you won't forget.
