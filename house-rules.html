House Rules:

Misgunst Boerdery is very excited to host you as our guests. We are members of the Conservancy, so please take note of the following:

  • Turn off loud music by 10:00 PM.
  • Dispose of all rubbish, including cigarette butts, in the bins provided.
  • Full garbage bags should be placed in the waste storage area by the wooden steps leading down to the built facilities.
  • If the wind is very strong, no fires please, as it is a fire hazard.
  • Pet owners: Please supervise your pet at all times, no excessive barking, and clean up after your pet.
  • Fireworks and Chinese lanterns are prohibited.

General Arrangements:

  • Arrivals after 2:00 PM.
  • Departures before 10:00 AM.
  • Leave all facilities as you found them.
  • Please drive VERY slowly and carefully in the camping area, as there are hidden entrances, children, and elderly persons.

Cottages:

  • Please wash the dishes before you leave.
  • Empty the small trash bins before you leave.
