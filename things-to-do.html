Things To Do

4x4 Adventures

Discover the ultimate 4x4 adventure at Vleesbaai on the scenic Misgunst Farm. This 12 km coastal route offers breathtaking ocean views and the perfect challenge for off-road enthusiasts. Whether you're a seasoned driver or just looking for some family fun, our well-marked dunes promise an unforgettable experience.

Ready for the challenge?

Explore the Oystercatcher Hiking Trail

Embark on the Oystercatcher Slackpack Trail, where you'll walk along the stunning South Cape coastline, crossing beaches, dunes, and cliffs. This guided trail offers an unforgettable adventure for nature lovers.

Ready to discover this breathtaking hike?

Bird Watching at Misgunst Farm

Discover a birdwatcher's paradise at Voëlvlei Pan, where diverse bird species thrive in the heart of nature. Whether you're exploring by vehicle or stopping along the shoreline, the area promises an unforgettable experience for bird enthusiasts.

Keen to spot rare and migratory birds?

Fishing at Fransmanshoek Conservancy

Experience exceptional fishing in the stunning Fransmanshoek Conservancy, where rugged cliffs meet deep waters. With famous spots like Die Saal and Verleëklip, anglers can reel in catches of shark, cob, garrick, and more.

Ready for your next big catch?

Explore the Marine Life of Vleesbaai

Vleesbaai is home to a stunning array of marine life, from playful dolphins and migrating whales to shy Oystercatcher birds and fascinating creatures in the intertidal zone. Whether you're walking the shore or exploring tide pools, there's always something remarkable to discover.

Curious about the marine life in Vleesbaai?

Experience the Thrill of Dragon Dune Sandboarding

Welcome to Dragon Dune, South Africa's biggest and longest sandboard ride! Perfect for all ages and skill levels, this adventure promises unforgettable fun. Whether you’re standing, sitting, or belly-boarding, the smooth, private slopes guarantee an exhilarating experience.

Ready for the ride of a lifetime?
