Experience the Untouched Beauty of Misgunst Farm

Surrounded by pristine dunes, rugged coastlines, and vast natural landscapes, Misgunst Farm offers an idyllic escape for nature lovers and families. Situated within the Fransmanshoek Conservancy, this eco-friendly farm invites you to explore its breathtaking 4x4 dune route, immerse yourself in the peaceful surroundings, and connect with nature.

Unwind at Coba-Cottage

Experience total relaxation at Coba-Cottage, an off-the-grid cottage on Misgunst Farm. With solar power, stunning views, and endless nearby adventures, this eco-friendly escape is perfect for nature lovers. Inquire today to reserve your spot at this tranquil retreat.

Experience Nature at Andries's Nook

Located on Misgunst Farm, Andries's Nook offers a rustic, eco-friendly retreat perfect for nature lovers and adventure seekers. With serene beaches, hiking trails, and 4x4 routes nearby, this cozy cabin is the perfect base for relaxation and exploration. Embrace the Outdoors at Hendrik's Camps

Discover Hendrik, a picturesque camping retreat on Misgunst Farm, where you can pitch your tent or park your caravan and immerse yourself in nature. Enjoy breathtaking views, solar-powered amenities, and a host of outdoor adventures, all while reconnecting with family and friends. Book your camping experience today and embrace the beauty of the great outdoors!
